Push-up
A bodyweight exercise that targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps. Perform by lowering your body to the ground and pushing back up, keeping the body in a straight line.
Squat
A lower body exercise that works the quads, hamstrings, and glutes. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, lower the hips as if sitting in a chair, and return to standing.
Plank
A core exercise where you hold a push-up position on your forearms and toes, keeping the body in a straight line to engage the abs, back, and shoulders.
Lunges
A lower-body exercise that targets the quads, glutes, and hamstrings. Step forward into a lunge position, lowering your back knee toward the ground, then return to standing.
Deadlift
A weightlifting exercise that targets the hamstrings, glutes, and lower back. With feet hip-width apart, bend at the hips to lower the barbell or dumbbells to the ground, then stand back up.
Mountain Climbers
A full-body, cardio-intensive exercise that engages the core, shoulders, and legs. From a plank position, alternate bringing your knees toward your chest quickly, as if "climbing."
